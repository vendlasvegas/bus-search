How to Filter for Your Bus Number:

  1. Click the button above to open the Excel file
  2. Sign in with your Microsoft account if prompted
  3. Once Excel opens, click on the Data tab in the ribbon
  4. Click the Filter button (funnel icon)
  5. Click the dropdown arrow on Column F (Bus Number)
  6. Type your bus number in the search box or find it in the list
  7. Uncheck "Select All" and check only your bus number
  8. Click OK to apply the filter
  9. You'll now see only rows with your selected bus number

Note: You must have permission to access the Excel file.
